Hi everyone! 👋 I'm so excited to share my story with you! A lot of people ask me how I started my lip gloss business when I'm only 8 years old, so I thought I'd tell you all about it!

## It All Started with Sparkles ✨

I've always LOVED anything that sparkles and shines. Glitter, sequins, shimmery lip gloss - if it's sparkly, I'm interested! One day I was watching my mom put on her lip gloss before we went to the grocery store, and I thought "I could make that even more beautiful!"

I asked my mom if we could try making lip gloss together, and she said "Sure, but only if we can find a recipe that's safe for you to make." So we spent a whole afternoon looking up recipes online!

## Learning the Science 🧪

Did you know making lip gloss is actually science? It's true! You have to get the measurements exactly right or it won't work. My first attempt was... well, let's just say it didn't turn out great! 😅

But my mom taught me that failing is just part of learning. So we tried again, and again, and again. Each time I got a little better at:

- Measuring ingredients exactly
- Mixing everything at the right temperature  
- Pouring into the little containers without spilling
- Testing to make sure it tasted good!

## My First Customer 💕

After I made about 10 batches and they were all turning out perfect, my neighbor Mrs. Johnson asked if she could buy one! I was SO excited - my first real customer!

I charged her $5 (which seemed like a lot of money to me at the time), and she loved it so much that she told all her friends. Pretty soon I had more orders than I could keep up with!

## Growing My Business 📈

That's when my mom helped me think about this like a real business. We figured out:

- **How much ingredients cost** - I had to make sure I wasn't losing money!
- **How long each lip gloss takes to make** - so I could plan my time
- **How to package them nicely** - first impressions matter!
- **How to keep track of orders** - I have a special notebook just for this

Now I make lip gloss every weekend and after school. I have my own little workspace set up in our kitchen, and I've gotten so good at it that I can make 20 lip glosses in one afternoon!

## What I've Learned About Business 🎯

Running a business has taught me so much! Here are my biggest lessons:

**1. Quality matters more than speed** - It's better to make one amazing lip gloss than five okay ones.

**2. Customers are the most important part** - I always try to make my customers happy, even if it means extra work.

**3. Organization is super important** - I have to keep track of inventory, orders, and money. My parents help me with the hard math!

**4. You have to be brave** - Sometimes I get nervous talking to new customers, but being brave helps my business grow.

**5. Saving money is smart** - I put half of what I earn into savings for college!

## Dreams for the Future 🌟

I have so many dreams for my business! Someday I want to:

- Have my own store (maybe online and in real life!)
- Create 50 different flavors
- Help other kids start their own businesses
- Maybe even sell my lip glosses in real stores!

But for now, I'm just happy making beautiful lip glosses that make people smile. Every time someone tells me they love their lip gloss, it makes all the hard work worth it!

## You Can Do It Too! 💪

If you're a kid reading this and thinking "I want to start a business too," here's my advice:

1. **Pick something you REALLY love** - For me it's sparkly things, but maybe for you it's drawing, or baking, or building things!

2. **Ask a grown-up to help** - My parents have been amazing helpers and teachers.

3. **Start small** - I didn't try to make 100 lip glosses on my first day. I started with just one!

4. **Don't give up when things go wrong** - Trust me, things will go wrong sometimes. That's normal!

5. **Have fun!** - If you're not having fun, what's the point?
